2Market Customer Purchase Behavior

Analyzed demographics, purchasing patterns, and ad effectiveness for a global supermarket using SQL, Tableau, and R. Key insights revealed that high-income, highly educated, older customers are the top spenders, with Instagram driving the most ad conversions. Built predictive models explaining 73% of spending variance, supporting targeted campaigns and data-driven marketing strategies.
