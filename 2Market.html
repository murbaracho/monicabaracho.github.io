2Market Customer Purchase Behavior

Data Analytics Case Study by Monica Baracho

Project Overview

The 2Market project explores customer demographics, purchase behavior, and campaign strategy. I used R, SQL, Tableau, and Excel to create dashboards and analyze patterns that help guide marketing decisions.

Key Findings

Three interactive Tableau dashboards were developed to present key customer insights clearly and engagingly.

Demographics & Spending Patterns highlights:
  • The average customer age is ~55 years (or 44 years if using 2014 as base).
  • Married individuals are the largest segment (~857 customers, 38.7%), followed by 'together' (573, 26%).
  • Positive correlation between age, income, and education.
  • Master’s and PhD holders have the highest average spending.
  • Liquor and non-vegetables are the most purchased categories among higher-educated, married customers.
High-Income Segment Analysis ($90K–$100K):

Customers around 53 years old, especially single/divorced, spend the most. Instagram is the top-performing ad channel. Households without young kids buy more premium products like liquor, chocolates, and meat—great opportunity for premium Instagram campaigns.

Country Spend & Ad Summary:
  • Spain leads total spend ($659,557) and social revenue unique % (36%).
  • Canada and South Africa follow at 31% each.
  • Montenegro shows no social media conversions.
