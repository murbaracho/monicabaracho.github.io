2Market Project

Data Analytics Case Study by Monica Baracho

Project Overview

The 2Market project explores customer demographics, purchase behavior, and campaign strategy. I used R, SQL, and Tableau to create dashboards and analyze patterns that help guide marketing decisions.

Key Insights

  • Average customer age ~55 years; married customers dominate the segment (~39%).
  • Positive correlation between age, income, and education.
  • PhD/Master’s customers show the highest average spend.
  • Instagram is the top ad channel for high-income customers ($90–$100K).
  • Spain leads with highest total spend and social ad conversion.

Tools Used

R, SQL, Tableau, Excel
